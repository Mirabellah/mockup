Frequently asked questions?

  • The dog sitter offers a range of services tailored to meet the individual needs of each furry client. These services typically include feeding, walking, playtime, potty breaks, administering medication if needed, and providing plenty of love and attention.

  • We have extensive experience working with a variety of dog breeds, sizes, and temperaments. From energetic puppies to senior dogs needing extra care, we've encountered and cared for them all. The experience has taught us how to adapt my approach to meet the unique needs of each dog, ensuring they feel comfortable, loved, and well-cared for during their time with me. For further information please contact the individual sitter.

  • In the rare event of an emergency while your pet is under our care, we have established protocols in place to ensure their safety and well-being. This includes that the sitter gets into contact with you immediate or your designated emergency contact, as well as prompt communication with veterinary professionals if necessary.

  • It's recommended to book pet sitting services as far in advance as possible to ensure availability, especially during peak times like holidays or vacation seasons. Generally, booking at least a few weeks ahead is advisable, but many dogsitters strive to accommodate last-minute requests whenever possible.
