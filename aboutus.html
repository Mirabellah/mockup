Mission

Our mission is to redefine pet sitting by providing a nurturing, safe, and personalized experience where a guest takes care of the pet.

Vision

Our vision is to transform the way pet owners experience peace of mind when they're away from their beloved companions. We envision a world where every pet owner can confidently pursue their commitments knowing that their furry family members are in the best hands possible.

Values

Customer transparency

Transparency is necessary to create a sense of safety between pet owners, pet sitters and their pets.

Bringing together local pet owners

Create a local pet community where our audience can communicate and share their passion for pets.

Pet, pet owner and pet sitter wellbeing

We work hard to ensure the wellbeing of our audience which is pets, pet owners and pet sitters. We want to create a comfortable and safe experience.

Unleashing Our Tale 

Are you seeking trustworthy and loving care for your furry family members? Look no further! At Sit&Stay, we understand that your pets are not just animals, they're cherished members of your family. We're here to provide them with the love, attention, and care they deserve when you can't be there
